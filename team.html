Our Team

Robot X is a hardworking, motivated and productive team. Last year was our first year as a team, so we worked hard and earned the highest rookie seed award. This year we are determined to do even better. The most defining quality of our team is communication, which is a key part of succeeding as a team. Without it, nothing would ever get done and making fatal mistakes would be inevitable. On our team, everybody is willing to do what is necessary to get the job done.
